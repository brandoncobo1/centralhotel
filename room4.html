Book a Room — Sea View

The Book a Room is the hotel's finest room. A separate bedroom. A private bathroom that doesn't rush you. Soundproof walls so the city stays outside where it belongs. A flat-screen TV with streaming services for evenings you choose to spend in. And a sea view — the Adriatic, framed by your window, from the moment you open the curtains.

This is the room guests book for anniversaries, for the nights they want to remember. The minibar is stocked with care. The welcome bottle of local wine is already waiting. All the other rooms build toward what the Book a Room delivers — and it delivers it completely.
